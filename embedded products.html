Overview

The “Marker light driver” is a switch mode power supply to provide the constant power source for marker light at the maximum brightness. Gathering high voltage driving application the product is intended with power control of the load including short circuit and over load protection. The constant voltage at the outputs is used to achieve the high performance and good efficiency of the driver. It can also be used in many more applications.


Key Features

  • Universal input voltage range (85 VDC to 265 VDC)
  • Dual outputs maximize LED strings and reduce system solution size, cost and complexity
  • High breakdown voltage of 700 V provides excellent input surge withstand
  • System level thermal overload, output short-circuit and open control loop protection
  • High thermal shutdown temperature (+135 °C minimum)
  • Small driver solutions for space-constrained applications
  • Excellent line and load regulation even with typical configuration
  • Built-in current limit and hysteretic thermal protection

Applications

  • Used for Indoor or Outdoor Lightning
  • Used where non-directional light is required

Mechanical Specification

  • Board size 103.250mm x 40mm x 91.650mm
  • Board thickness 2mm (0.078 inch)

Overview

Motor Controller card is used to control the brushed DC Motor (24V DC motor). This can handle DC motor with any kind of high RPM motor. This can be used in ay system where in can be controlled by the simple/ advanced controller cards as per the customer’s requirement/application. DC - Motor Controller is designed for 24V permanent magnet motors with brushes in the power range of 5-70W (0.2 – 3.0 A).


Key Features

  • Dual Direction. (Can move the motor in any direction)
  • Very less response time
  • Continuous / 2-step speed control
  • Adjustable acceleration / braking ramp
  • Adjustable current limit
  • Adjustable load compensation
  • Supply voltage variation compensated
  • High efficiency & low thermal loss

  • Supply 24 VDC
  • Idle current approx. - 100mA
