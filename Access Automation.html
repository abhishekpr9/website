Tripod Turnstile

Tripod Turnstile Gates are used to manage passages by working with different kind of Identification technologies. It can be integrated with Different access control system, thus safe, orderly and improved passing means are realized and unauthorized access is controlled. Our Gates provide a modular, extensible and flexible design with the security; reliability and high performance throughput required for mass transit systems where there is large flow of people. Our turnstiles are designed to meet most demanding needs of our customers. Available in Normally Closed (N/C) configuration to lock the mechanism until a valid authorization signal is received, or available in Normally Open (N/O) configuration to lock the mechanism in the event of attempted unauthorized entry. The N/O configuration allows also reduced power consumption and increases the life of the mechanism and passage through put speed. In case of emergency the hub freely rotates for an easier exit. You can also choose the drop arm option, which automatically drops the horizontal arm to allow free passage.

Flap Barrier/ Flap Gates

Flap Gates are used to manage passages by working with different kind of Identification technologies. It can be integrated with Different access control system, thus safe, orderly and improved passing means are realized and unauthorized access is controlled. Our Gates provide a modular, extensible and flexible design with the security; reliability and high performance throughput required for mass transit systems where there is large flow of people. Our Flap Gates are designed to meet most demanding needs of our customers. Available in Normally Closed (N/C) configuration to close the flap wings until a valid authorization signal is received, or available in Normally Open (N/O) configuration to close the flap wing in the event of attempted unauthorized entry. The N/O configuration allows also reduced power consumption and increases the life of the mechanism and passage through put speed. Attempt to cheat by unauthorized persons, e.g. passage in the wrong direction, or an attempt by a second person to follow without authorization (tailgating) is recognized and activates the alarm system. In the event of a power failure, the flap wings are opened automatically by springs. If the emergency inputs are activated, a hardware circuit shuts down the end-stage, the motors are de-energized and the flaps open.

Boom Barrier

Boom Barrier offered provides for rapid opening that makes these ideally suited for high-volume traffic areas. Further, the barrier system also comes with provision of battery backup in form of 12V battery driven motor that ensures all time operation availability with no comprise done on the security front. In no power conditions, the provision of built-in mains failure detection allows keeping barriers in raised conditions. Other than these, the high-torque boom pole operations through use of DC motor and reduction gearbox ensures consistent functioning of the boom barrier.
